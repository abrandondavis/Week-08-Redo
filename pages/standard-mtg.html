Overview of Standard Format:

Standard is a dynamic format in Magic: The Gathering that consistently attracts players for several compelling reasons. In Standard, players construct decks using cards exclusively from the most recent sets, typically encompassing the last two years of expansions and core sets. This format's popularity stems from its ever-evolving nature, promoting adaptability and innovation as the card pool continuously changes. With each new set release, players must refine their strategies, keeping the metagame fresh and exciting.
